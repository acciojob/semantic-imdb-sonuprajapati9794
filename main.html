Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's subconscious during their dreams. He is offered a chance to regain his old life if he can successfully perform "inception" — planting an idea into someone's mind.

Actors

  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
